<h1><b>E-COMMERCE SALES & DELIVERY DASHBOARD | POWER-BI</b></h1> 
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ShopNestâ€™s e-commerce performance across product categories, delivery trends, payment behaviors, customer ratings, and regional sales. The goal of this project was to transform the raw retail dataset into actionable insights for business decision-making.

<h2><b>Project Overview:</b></h2>

- Top categories by total sales.
- Monthly delayed vs. on-time deliveries.
- Payment method distribution.
- Product rating analysis.
- State-wise revenue contribution.
- Seasonal and annual revenue patterns.
- Delayed order analysis by product category.

<h2><b>Process and Approach:</b></h2>

**1. Data Collection:**
- Imported the raw dataset provided for the ShopNest store.
- Reviewd missing values, duplicate data and business requirements.

**2. Data Cleaning and Transformation:**
- Removed duplicates and null records.
- Standardized product categories, delivery status, and payment types.
- Formatted dates, states, and numeric fields.
- Created additional calculated columns where required.

**3. Data Modeling:**
- Built a star-schema style model to optimize relationships.
- Ensured proper one-to-many relationships between fact and dimension tables.
- Applied data normalization for faster queries.

**4. DAX Calculations:**
Created key performance measures including:
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Delayed Orders
- Top Rated / Low Rated Products
- Quarterly and Yearly Revenue
- Category-level by Total sales.

<h2><b>Key Insights:</b></h2>

- **Top Sales Categories:** Health & Beauty are the top categories with total sales of **$1.53M**
- **Delayed Orders:** Over 44,000+ delays recorded, with the highest delays in bed/bath/table categories.
- **Delivery Performance:** Noticeable month-to-month fluctuations between delayed and on-time orders.
- **Payment Behavior:** Credit card transactions dominate, followed by e-wallet and debit card payments.
- **Ratings:** Premium and lifestyle products rank among the highest-rated items, while certain home and toy items fall into the lowest-rated group.
- **Regional Sales:** States such as RJ, SP, and MG show the highest total sales, indicating strong customer activity in these regions.
- **Revenue Patterns:** Strong seasonal spikes observed, and yearly revenue shows consistent growth in 2017 and 2018.
